  • This piece was made for the first project in my Digital Painting class at Illinois State University.
  • For this project we had to take multiple images of lur choice and combine them to make our own art piece by tracing over them.
  • The images that I had chosen were one of the night sky, one of the Darvaza gas crater, and one of each form of the Pokemon Houndoom.
  • I combined these images inorder to make a Houndoom version of Cerberus guarding the Gates of Hell at night.
  • For coloring this piece I decided to try to stick with the more typical Pokemon coloring style of cel shading.
