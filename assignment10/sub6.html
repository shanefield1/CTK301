  • This was done for the final project for my Digital Painting class.
  • The project was to take something that has not been shown visually before whether it be from a song, poem, or book and make an art piece of that.
  • My piece is of the scene where Grand Admiral Thrawn dies at the end of the book Star Wars: The Last Command by Timothy Zahn the final book of the Thrawn Trilogy.
  • Due to my fondness of the Art style used in Hazbin Hotel and Helluva Boss I did my best to make this piece in that art style.
